Sound Aides is an archival of sound clips specifically designed to be modded into games that assist otherwise blinded players through every section of a game with newly created sound clips specifically designed for such an occassion.

Sound Aides aims to aid gamers with vision defects with:
+Guidelines that help guide players through their decisions made in games.
+Help gamers have a bigger memory foothold in a prolonged game session.
+Help remind gamers of their recent actions through verbal cues.
